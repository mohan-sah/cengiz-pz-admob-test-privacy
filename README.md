# cengiz-pz-admob-test-privacy


Privacy Policy for cengiz-pz-admob-test

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how cengiz-pz-admob-test ("the Game") handles your information. The Game is built using the Godot Engine and utilizes the "Cengiz" AdMob plugin to provide advertisements and monetization features.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a better gameplay experience, we may collect the following information:

User-Provided & Technical Data

Usernames: We may collect usernames that are either randomly generated by the Game or provided by you. These are used primarily for displaying High Scores within the game environment.

High Scores: We track gameplay progress and scores to facilitate competitive features and local leaderboards.

Device Identifiers: We may access unique device IDs (such as the Android ID or Advertising ID) for internal analytics and to ensure the proper functioning of the Game.

In-Game Currency: The Game tracks "tokens" used for hints. These can be earned via ads or purchased.

Note: Currently, this data is primarily processed locally on your device. We do not maintain a private external database for permanent storage of personal identity at this time.

Third-Party Service Providers (Google AdMob)

The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including:

IP addresses

Advertising IDs (e.g., Google Advertising ID)

Device information (model, OS version)

App usage and interaction data

For more details, please review Google's Privacy & Terms.

2. Advertising & Monetization

Our Game shows various types of advertisements to keep content accessible. These formats include:

Banner & Native Advanced Ads: Inline ads that match the game's look.

Interstitial & App Open Ads: Full-page ads at transitions or during app startup.

Rewarded & Rewarded Interstitial Ads: Ads that grant you in-game tokens/currency for viewing.

Ad-Free Option (In-App Purchases)

The Game may offer a "Remove Ads" feature. If you choose to pay a one-time fee to stop advertisements, the transaction is handled entirely by the Google Play Store. We do not see, process, or store your credit card or payment information.

3. Children's Privacy

Our Game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete this from our records immediately.

4. Security

We value your trust. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
